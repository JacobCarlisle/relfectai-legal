Common Topics

Subscription help

  • Purchases, cancellations, and renewals are managed by Apple.
  • To manage a subscription, open the App Store subscription settings on your device.

Account deletion

  1. Open ReflectAI.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion request.

Deleting your account removes your ReflectAI account and associated cloud journal data.

Privacy questions

Review the Privacy Policy at [privacy policy URL].

Trouble signing in

  • Confirm you are using the correct email address.
  • Check that your internet connection is active.
  • Try signing in again after a short delay.

Audio or transcription issues

  • Confirm microphone permission is enabled in iOS Settings.
  • Make sure the recording is clear and long enough to transcribe.
  • Retry on a stable network connection.
